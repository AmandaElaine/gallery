About me

My name is Amanda Hinton and I am a junior at Virginia Tech. Virginia Tech was not my first choice when it came to picking a college. That is, until I came to visit the campus for the first time. It was my senior year of high school and the time to make my college decision was getting close. I was set on going to JMU with my best friend, but my dad insisted I visit Virginia Tech before I made a decision. It might sound cheesy, but it was love at first sight. The moment I stepped foot on campus I just knew. Virginia Tech was where I was meant to be. It has been three years since my decision, and I can honestly say I have never regretted it- not even for a second.
